What a way to do! You don’t allow your customers to stand in a queue and wait for staff to take orders or wait to pay for the bills.

How it Works?

Scan QR-code

Guest Scan's QR Code placed on the table with his / her mobile phone. The QR code will available on every table.

Take Order

Guest browse's the menu without downloading any app. Select all his/her favourites and make a payment.

Accept Order

As waiters receive orders or service callings, they accept it and sent for preparation.

Order Delivery

Customers receive the orders faster with accurate service and they can re-order food.
