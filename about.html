About

TITAN

TITAN is an innovation to make services intelligent, accurate, and easy in the field of Food and Beverages.

Customers always look for a better service, maintain kings and queens attitude, and want to stay within their comfort zones. Yes, TITAN has it all – orders are easy, need not be standing in queue, and interaction with waiters are not necessary. It makes the services more customer-oriented and creates customer satisfaction. What more do you need than to make your guests happy?

TITAN comprises of a young motivated professionals and a promising team who grows with the pace of time. So too, is the innovation and idea of TITAN.

Why would your clients like Titan?

  • Without registering and login, it creates a provision to get into your menu with a simple scan from their mobile phone with photos and detailed description.
  • They can access your current promotions, special and Happy Hours offers.
  • The self-direct ordering and service calling system saves time for the customers and improves service quality.
  • It has zero chances of security breach.
  • It is free and easy to use.
Why would you like Titan?

  • You can configure TITAN according to your requirements – personalize the application by defining your logo and background images; set required languages and working time.
  • Managing the digital menu is quick and easy – you can create, edit or remove items and set images with a few clicks; promotion and special offers are client-friendly; make changes in digital menu up-to-date without additional costs.
  • By saving time and avoiding errors, it can increase number of orders, and improves the work flow. In turn, it maximizes revenue.
  • To avoid any untoward incidence happened, you can easily check customer’s full history of orders and payment made.
  • You can used it as a tool for supplementing as well as controlling the staff’s work.
Why would your staffs like Titan?

  • As the customers give direct order using mobile app, your staffs reduce idle running to tables.
  • As the process of order waste no time for staffs, they have sufficient time to focus on quick and quality service.
  • It eliminates errors – no more missed orders, delayed services, or related confusions.
